Rugged landscapes, exotic music, and adventure travels have shaped my life. The freedom inherent in these themes inspires me to design fluid and clean technologies that live and breathe through user experience.





ABOUT MY WORK


For nearly a decade I have made it my purpose to travel the world, live creatively, and explore hidden places.

A strong background in linguistic and fine art studies has informed my approach to programming as the verbal sculpture of reality. My travels have taught me how to grow and change gracefully within the ever-evolving world of web development.

I treat each project as unique and dynamic, honing in on the humanity and personality of technology as a powerful communication tool.

I work primarily in JavaScript, Express, and Ruby on Rails.
